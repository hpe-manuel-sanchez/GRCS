Free Hand Company Management

* This is applicable only for Regular/Non-Traditional Project.

To add Third Party company, please visit Third party section. Requestor adds a free hand third party company and submits a project.

1. After adding a free hand third party company and submitting a project, System triggers an email to the GTA whenever the first resource on the project is approved

2. Once the Company gets created in GT and requestor navigates to project from inbox hyperlink.

3. Click “Remove link” against the free hand company in third party section.

4. Search for the third party company based on one or more combination of search criteria. For Description: Please refer Third Party Section
