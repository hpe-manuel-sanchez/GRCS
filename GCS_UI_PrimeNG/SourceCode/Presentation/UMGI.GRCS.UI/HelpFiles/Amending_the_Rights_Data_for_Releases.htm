Amending the Rights Data for Releases

Only users with Edit Rights data (Header), Edit Rights data (Digital),Edit Rights data (Secondary), Edit Rights data (Pre-Cleared), Edit Rights Review Status privileges can search, view and Amend the rights data created for a release (s) in GRS. Using the Custom Rights Review WQ  user can review/amend the rights for required releases.

  1. Select Rights Administration -> Rights Review Work Queue-> Create Custom Settings -> Releases. The Custom Setting page is displayed. By default, the ‘Custom Parameter’ Tab is displayed. You can enter one or a combination of criteria to create the Custom Work queue for the releases.

 

  1. User can review the Rights  Data Sets in 2 Tabs:

    • Rights Acquired  Tab

    • Digital Restrictions Tab

Rights Acquired Tab

 

  1. Rights Period:- You click on individual row item(s) and the following values can be edited to

    • In Perpetuity

    • Life of Copyright

    • Other Duration

  1. Lost Rights:- You can update the value to ‘Yes/No.

  2. Lost Rights Date:- You click on individual row item and then the calendar value can be edited

  3. Lost Rights Reason:- You click on individual row item(s) and the following values can be edited to

    • Rights Expiry

    • Rights Reversion

    • Litigation

  1. Rights Exception:- You click on individual row item and the following values can be edited to

    • Exception Applied

    • Exception Not Applied

  1. Physical Rights flag:- You can update the value to ‘Yes/No'

  2. Digital Rights Flag:- You can update the value to ‘Yes/No’

Digital Restriction Tab

  1. Use Type:- You click on individual row item and the following values can be edited to

    • Download

    • Streaming

  1. Commercial Models:- You click on individual row item and the following values can be edited to

    • A la carte

    • Subscription

    • Ad-Funded

    • All

  1. Restrictions:-You click on individual row item and the following values can be edited to

    • No Rights

    • Consent Required

    • Refer to legal

    • No Restrictions

    • Consult

  1. Restriction Reason:- You click on individual row item and the following values can be edited to

    • Artist Consent Required

    • Sample Issues

    • Side Artist Issues

    • Missing “No Rights”

    • Other

  1. Consent Period:- You click on individual row item and the following values can be edited to

    • During Hold back Period

    • During Term

    • During & After Term
