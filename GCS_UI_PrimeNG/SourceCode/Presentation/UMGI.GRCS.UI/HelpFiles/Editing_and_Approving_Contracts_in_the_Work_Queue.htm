Editing and Approving Contracts in the Work Queue

You can edit and/or approve contracts based on your role and the work flow status of the contracts.

  1. In the Contract Maintenance grid, select the check box corresponding to the contract row to edit/approve.

  2. Click Edit Contract. The contract is opened & the Edit Contract screen is displayed.


      

  1. Edit / review the contract details as required and click Save.

  2. To approve the contract, select Approved from the Workflow Status drop-down and click Save.


     

The contract is approved.
